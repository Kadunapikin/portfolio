About Me

My Work History

For seven years, I dedicated myself to the agricultural loan sector, holding key roles at LAPO and Letshego Micro Finance Banks. I pioneered loan products that elevated the income of SMEs and rural farmers, boosted agricultural productivity, and funneled more financial resources into agriculture. Additionally, I was instrumental in modernizing rural farming practices, strengthening Nigeria's agricultural value chain.

Currently, I volunteer at TOP Software Developers in Kaduna, Nigeria. I play an active role in team projects, collaborating with other front-end developers for seamless project deployment on GitHub. As a team leader, I delegate tasks, offer feedback, and ensure smooth operations.

My Personal History

Having graduated in 2013 with a Diploma in Agricultural Extension from Nuhu Bamalli Polytechnic, I joined LAPO MFB in 2015. My commitment earned me the Best Client Support Officer Award in 2017. By 2019, I was leading agricultural teams for Letshego MFB across three states. However, drawn to the tech world, I shifted my focus in 2022 to pursue Front End Development with TOP Software Developers, setting my sights on becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer.

Currently mastering the MERN-Stack (MongoDB, Express, React JS, Node JS) at TOP Software Developers Nigeria, with a parallel focus on remotely mentoring budding developers to reach their tech aspirations.
